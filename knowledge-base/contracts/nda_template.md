# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Template

## Parties

**Disclosing Party**: [Company Name]  
**Receiving Party**: [Party Name]  
**Effective Date**: [Date]

## Purpose

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the parties for the purpose of allowing the Receiving Party to evaluate potential business opportunities with the Disclosing Party.

## Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, whether orally, in writing, or in any other form, including but not limited to:

- Business plans, strategies, and financial information
- Technical data, know-how, and trade secrets
- Product specifications and designs
- Customer lists and information
- Marketing plans and strategies
- Software, algorithms, and source code
- Any other information marked as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"

## Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:
1. Was publicly known at the time of disclosure
2. Becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement
3. Was rightfully known by the Receiving Party prior to disclosure
4. Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information
5. Is rightfully received from a third party without breach of any confidentiality obligation

## Obligations of Receiving Party

The Receiving Party agrees to:
1. Hold and maintain all Confidential Information in strict confidence
2. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose stated in this Agreement
3. Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
4. Take reasonable precautions to protect the confidentiality of the information
5. Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon request or termination of discussions

## Term

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of [X] years from the Effective Date, or until terminated by either party with written notice.

## Return of Materials

Upon termination of this Agreement or upon request, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all documents, materials, and copies containing Confidential Information.

## Remedies

The parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm, and the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity.

## General Provisions

- **Governing Law**: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [State/Country]
- **Entire Agreement**: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties
- **Modification**: Any modification must be in writing and signed by both parties
- **Assignment**: This Agreement may not be assigned without prior written consent
