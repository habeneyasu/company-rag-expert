# Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to the Team!

This guide will help you navigate your first weeks at the company and set you up for success.

## Pre-First Day Checklist

### Before You Start
- [ ] Complete all pre-employment paperwork
- [ ] Review and sign employment agreement
- [ ] Set up direct deposit for payroll
- [ ] Review company policies and handbook
- [ ] Confirm start date and time with your manager
- [ ] Prepare questions about your role and team

## First Day Schedule

### Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- **9:00 AM**: Arrival and check-in at reception
- **9:15 AM**: Welcome session with HR
- **9:45 AM**: IT setup and equipment distribution
- **10:30 AM**: Office tour and introductions
- **11:00 AM**: Meet with your manager
- **11:30 AM**: Team introductions

### Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
- **1:00 PM**: Lunch with team
- **2:00 PM**: System access setup and training
- **3:00 PM**: Review role expectations and goals
- **4:00 PM**: Complete required training modules
- **4:30 PM**: Q&A session with HR

## First Week Goals

### Week 1 Objectives
1. Complete all required training
2. Set up all necessary accounts and access
3. Meet key stakeholders in your department
4. Understand your role and responsibilities
5. Review team processes and workflows
6. Schedule regular check-ins with your manager

### Required Training Modules
- Company policies and code of conduct
- IT security and data protection
- Diversity and inclusion
- Health and safety procedures
- Time tracking and expense reporting

## First Month Priorities

### Month 1 Goals
- Build relationships with team members
- Understand company culture and values
- Complete initial project assignments
- Attend department meetings and all-hands
- Set up development goals with manager
- Complete probationary period requirements

## Tips for Success

1. **Ask Questions**: Don't hesitate to ask for clarification
2. **Take Notes**: Document important information and processes
3. **Be Proactive**: Seek out opportunities to contribute
4. **Build Relationships**: Invest time in getting to know your colleagues
5. **Stay Organized**: Use tools and systems to manage your work
6. **Seek Feedback**: Regularly check in with your manager
7. **Be Patient**: Give yourself time to learn and adapt
