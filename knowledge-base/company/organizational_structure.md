## Organizational Principles

### Flat Structure
We maintain a relatively flat organizational structure to encourage communication, collaboration, and quick decision-making.

### Cross-Functional Teams
Many projects involve cross-functional teams with members from different departments working together toward common goals.

### Matrix Reporting
Some roles may have dual reporting relationships, particularly in project-based work or specialized initiatives.

### Growth and Scaling
As the company grows, we continuously evaluate and adjust our organizational structure to maintain efficiency and effectiveness.
