# Product FAQ

## General Questions

### What products do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive suite of enterprise software solutions including ERP, CRM, Business Intelligence, Project Management, and Collaboration tools. See our Product Catalog for detailed information.

### Do you offer free trials?
Yes, all products offer a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), ACH transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers.

### Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

### Do you offer discounts for annual subscriptions?
Yes, annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.

## Pricing and Billing

### How is pricing calculated?
Pricing is typically based on the number of users, with some products offering flat-rate pricing. Enterprise plans include custom pricing based on your specific needs.

### Are there setup fees?
No setup fees for Standard and Professional plans. Enterprise implementations may include professional services fees.

### What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
You'll be notified when approaching limits and can upgrade your plan or purchase additional capacity. Some limits may result in service restrictions.

### Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in. What should I do?
1. Check that you're using the correct email and password
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Contact support if issues persist

### The system is running slowly. What can I do?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check our status page for known issues
5. Contact support if the issue continues

### I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?
Error messages include specific codes and descriptions. Check our Error Code Reference in the documentation, or contact support with the error code.
